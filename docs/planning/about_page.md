## Our Approach

### The Prediction Philosophy

**Everything is probabilistic.** We don't claim certainty‚Äîwe quantify likelihood. A 70% win probability means exactly that: seven times out of ten, we expect this outcome. The other three times matter just as much.

**Edge over perfection.** No model is 100% accurate. Success comes from being right more often than chance, and knowing when confidence is highest. We optimize for consistent edges, not impossible precision.

**Data first, intuition second.** Gut feelings don't scale. We let patterns emerge from data rather than imposing assumptions. When intuition conflicts with evidence, evidence wins.

**Transparency builds trust.** We share successes and failures. When models perform well, we explain why. When they underperform, we diagnose the issue and improve. Hiding mistakes doesn't make them disappear‚Äîit just delays learning.
